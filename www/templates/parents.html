Carpool Etiquette
To keep our children safe at pick-up and drop-off please follow these guidelines:
â€‹

1. The pick up zone is the area in front of the school where the railroad ties line the grass.

2. Drive up all the way to the end of the pick up zone and stay to the outside of the circle.

3. If a car in front of you drives away and you are still waiting for your child, please pull your car forward.

4. If your child is loaded into your car and another car in front of you is loading a child, please wait until they are loaded and pull away.

5. If you must drive around a car waiting for a child, please drive slowly and close to the inside of the circle. Please do not pass more than one car.

6. All traffic must go around the circle in a counterclockwise direction. Do not turn around and go against traffic.
