To enroll at Western Branch Academy a complete application packet must be submitted. Fall class assignment is based upon your child's age as of September 30, 2016. Summer class assignment is based upon your child's age as of June 2016. Children must be two by June 20th to enroll for Summer Camp.

An enrollment packet consists of the following documents:
​
1.
Registration Form
2.
Parental Permission and Tuition Agreement Form
3.
Enrollment Agreement Form
4.
Completed Physical
5.
Current Immunizations
6.
Proof of Identity(e.g. Birth Certificate)
7.
Fall Registration Fee ($110 1st child, $ 50 each additional child)
Summer Registration Fee ($75 1st child, $50 each additional child)
8.
School Year Activity Fee ($40-2 yo, $50-3 yo, $75-4 yo, and $100-Kindergarten)
Discounts Available:
​
1.
$10 Off Registration Fee (1st Child Only) when signing up for summer and fall at the same time
2.
2% discount given for tuition payments paying by Automatic (ACH) payments, monthly payments, or for the year in full
What to bring for the first day:
  • Registration Packet
  • Shot Record with Physical Form (Must receive within first 30 days of enrollment)
  • Birth Certificate
  • School supplies (Refer to Supply List)
  • Tote bag
  • Seasonal change of clothes with socks (Please place in labelled ziploc bag)
  • Nap map and crib sheet (full days students)
