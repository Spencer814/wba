Welcome to Western Branch Academy!
Thank you so much for voting for WBA in The Pilot's Best of Hampton Roads Contest! You have once again made us the Best Child Development Center in the Portsmouth Area!
The mission of Western Branch Academy is to believe in the abilities of young children; create a community for children to belong based on love, trust, and respect; and enable children to become creative thinkers with a love for learning.
We provide innovative preschool and Kindergarten programming for children ages two through six. We also provide Kindergarten and after school care for students enrolled at Chittum Elementary or Western Branch Primary. We are licensed as a child day center through the Virginia Department of Social Services.
