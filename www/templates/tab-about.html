About Us
Western Branch Academy is a full-day nationally accredited early childhood program that provides innovative programming for children where emotional, physical, intellectual, spiritual, and social development flourishes. We are committed to providing high quality instruction through age-appropriate learning activities and qualified teachers.
We strive to:
​

Support families through strong partnerships

Transform curiosity into a love of learning

Nurture each child's unique qualities and potential

Foster opportunities to learn about God's world

Establish a work environment where professionalism, growth, collaboration, and creativity are demonstrated

Connect with the community through community service projects

Meet the changing preschool educational needs of the community
